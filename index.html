What the MSCEIT®2 Is

An ability test, not a questionnaire

Unlike self-report questionnaires that measure how emotionally intelligent you believe you are, the MSCEIT®2 presents real emotional problems and scores your actual performance across four branches — perceiving emotions, connecting emotions to thinking, understanding emotions, and managing emotions. It was developed by leading researchers in the field and is published by MHS.

This four-branch science is the foundation SUMMS™ — the method in the book — is built on. Your assessment tells you where you're starting from; SUMMS™ tells you what to do next.

What you're buying: a debrief and development experience. Your assessment is administered under Zena's certification, and the heart of the engagement is a structured session where she interprets your profile with you and builds a development plan — including how your EI profile shapes the way you work with AI.

Your results belong to you

Individual MSCEIT®2 results are confidential to the participant. In team engagements, we report group patterns only; no one's individual scores are shared without their explicit written consent. Assessment data is never used for hiring, firing, or performance evaluation.

Why SCRIPT™ Is Different

It begins with intent and ends with testing

Plenty of prompt frameworks tell you to add context and assign a role. SCRIPT™ — Situation, Clear Intent, Role, Instructions, Parameters, Test — is distinctive for what it adds at both ends: declaring why you want the output, not just what, and evaluating the result against that intent. A complete, teachable practice, not a one-shot template.

Privacy & Responsible AI

Your trust is the whole business.
Here's how we protect it.

Emotional intelligence starts with respecting what people share with you. This statement explains, in plain language, what this site collects, what we do with it, and the commitments we make about using AI responsibly. Effective July 8, 2026.

What This Site Collects

Only what you choose to send us

This website sets no cookies, runs no advertising trackers, and uses no analytics that profile you. If you simply read and leave, we learn nothing about you personally.

If you submit the speaking enquiry form or join the email list, the details you type — your name, organization, email address, and message — are delivered to us by a form-relay service (FormSubmit) and arrive in our inbox at hello@grandstrategy.llc. Like most websites, our hosting provider (GitHub Pages) and font provider (Google Fonts) may record standard technical logs such as IP addresses when the page loads; we never see or use those logs.

What We Do With It

We reply. That's it.

What you send us is used to respond to you and, if we work together, to serve you well. We do not sell, rent, or trade your information. We do not add you to a mailing list you didn't ask to join. If you ask us to delete what we hold about you, we will, and we'll confirm when it's done — email hello@grandstrategy.llc any time to ask what we have, correct it, or remove it.

Assessment Confidentiality

Your MSCEIT®2 results are yours

Emotional intelligence assessment results are among the most personal data we handle, and they are treated that way. MSCEIT®2 assessments are administered through the secure, authenticated systems of the test publisher (MHS) — never through this website. Individual results are shared only with the individual in a confidential debrief. In team sessions we discuss patterns, never named individual scores. Assessment data is never used for marketing and never entered into public AI tools.

Responsible AI Commitment

We practice what the book teaches

We use AI tools in our own work — that's the point of the business — and we hold that use to the standard we teach. AI assists with drafting and research; a human reviews, decides, and sends. Nothing leaves this practice on autopilot. Client-confidential material and assessment results are never placed into public AI tools. And no decision about a person — a client, a participant, a student — is ever delegated to an AI system.

Schools & young people: our work with schools is designed for educators. This website does not knowingly collect information from children, and our school programs never require student personal data to flow through us.

Questions about any of this — or anything we could do better? hello@grandstrategy.llc. A person reads it, and a person answers.
